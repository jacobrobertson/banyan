Echinocystis is a genus in the gourd family Cucurbitaceae which includes the species Echinocystis lobata, commonly called wild cucumber or prickly cucumber. Some authors include the manroots(genus: Marah) in Echinocystis.

Echinocystis lobata is an annual vine, native to North America.
