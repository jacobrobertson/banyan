Taxonavigation[edit]

Regnum: Plantae

Cladus: Eudicots
Cladus: Core eudicots
Ordo: Caryophyllales

Familia: Nepenthaceae
Genus: Nepenthes
Hybrid: Nepenthes x kinabaluensis

Name[edit]

Nepenthes × kinabaluensis S.Kurata, nomen nudum (Nepenthes of Mount Kinabalu: 64 (1976)), without latin description or type.

Hybrid Parentage: Nepenthes rajah Hook.f. × Nepenthes villosa Hook.f.

Collections: Sabah, Borneo. Mt. Kinabalu, above Kambarangoh/Kamburangau, 2400-2700 m, II 1910, Gibbs 4300 (l.c.).

IPNI Id: 603739-1

Nepenthes × kinabaluensis Kurata ex Kurata (Journal of the Insectivorous Plant Society 35: 65 (1984))
Holotype: below Paka Cave, Mount Kinabalu, Sabah, Borneo, Malaysia, 12. 3. 1974, S.Kurata 8100-a (NDC).

Nepenthes kinabaluensis Sh.Kurata ex J.H.Adam & C.C.Wilcock (Sarawak Museum Journal 71: 152 (1996))
Holotype: Mount Kinabalu summit trail, Sabah, Borneo, Malaysia, 6. 1. 1988, J.H.Adam 2423 (UKMS).

IPNI Id: 1000872-1
