Name[edit]

Halothamnus Jaub. & Spach (1845)

  • Type of genus: Halothamnus bottae Jaub. & Spach

Synonyms[edit]

  • Aellenia Ulbr. (1934)
    • (comprising Aellenia auricula and Aellenia lancifolia).
  • Salsola L. sect. Sphragidanthus Iljin (1936)
    • (comprising Salsola glauca, Salsola subaphylla, Salsola hispidula and Salsola iliensis)
  • Aellenia emend. Aellen (1950)
    • (comprising Aellenia auricula, Aellenia glauca, Aellenia hierochuntica, Aellenia iliensis and Aellenia subaphylla)
  • Halothamnus emend. Botsch. (1981)
    • (including 25 species)
