[edit] Name

Cucurbita argyrosperma subsp. argyrosperma var. mixta (Pangalo) nom. inval.

[edit] Basionym

Cucurbita mixta Pangalo, in Bjull. Prikl. Bot. vol. 23, 3, 258, 254. 1930.

[edit] Synonyms

  • Cucurbita argyrosperma subsp. argyrosperma var. stenosperma (Pangalo) L. Merrick & D. M. Bates, Baileya 23:96. 1989. nom. illeg.
  • Cucurbita argyrosperma var. stenosperma (Pangalo) Huber, 1867. nom. illeg.
  • Cucurbita mixta var. stenosperma Pangalo, Trudy Prikl. Bot. 23(3):258. 1930.

[edit] Notes

No valid combination seems to exist, however var. stenosperma cannot be used.
