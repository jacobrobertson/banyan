What are all the icons for?
  
 
 
and - Show the Menu
and - Go to Details - See a detailed view of the chosen species.
 
 
- Show Children - Will show all direct children of the chosen species.
- Show More Species - Will show a sampling of species under the chosen species.
 
 
- Hide Children - Closes all children of the chosen species.
- Close - Closes the chosen species, and all its children.
 
 
- Focus - Show the chosen species in a new tree with just its hierarchy showing.
and - Pinning Images - Makes that species' image show as part of the tree.
 
 
† - Indicates that the given species is extinct.
- Thumbnails - Hover over to see a larger pop-up image, or click to go to the details screen.
